Brake Options

Standard Brakes

The Porsche 911 GT3RS comes with two options for brakes. The standard brakes are 6-piston aluminium caliper brakes in the front and 4-piston aluminium caliper brakes in the back.

Porsche Ceramic Composite Brakes

The optional Porsche Cermanic Composite Brakes (PCCB) add around an extra $8,000 to the car. The PCCB are lighter and stronger due to the build consisting of cast iron and carbon fiber. They can stop the car from 100mph to 0mph in under 236 feet (that's good).
