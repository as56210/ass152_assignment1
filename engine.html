GT3 vs GT3RS

Specifications

The 911 GT3RS is the the redesigned version of the GT3. However, when it comes to the engine they are more similar than people think. Let's look at how the engines compare in the two cars.

GT3 GT3RS
Size 4.0-liter 4.0-liter
Transmission 6-speed Manual or 7-speed PDK Automatic 7-speed PDK Automatic
0-60 MPH 3.3s (M) and 2.7s (PDK) 2.7s (PDK)
Torque 332 (lb-ft) 342 (lb-ft)

These specs show that the GT3 and the GT3RS are similar in power output. The main differences between the engines are the transmission and torque.
